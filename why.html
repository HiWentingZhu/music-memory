Why this museum exists

I built this because music remembered before I did.

After my layoff in May 2026, I finally had space to streamline my life. In that pause, I realized how much I might forget: not just events, but the feelings, thoughts, and small details of past years.

A familiar app, an old self

One day, I opened QQ Music, an app I had used for years. I was only wondering whether my taste had changed.

But as I looked through old songs, memories started coming back: a mood, a room, a version of myself, a feeling I thought had disappeared.

That was when the idea became clear:

Time moves on. Details blur. But music remembers.

This is not a playlist, a chart, or a yearly recap. It is a collection of memory rooms built from the songs I returned to, the moods I carried, the artists that stayed close, and the moments I almost forgot.
