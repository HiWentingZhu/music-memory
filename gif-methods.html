Four ways the same room can become animated

These previews use the approved 2025 room image. They show the kind of output each workflow tends to produce, before choosing a real export pipeline.

1. FFmpeg + scripted frames

Controlled motion

Best when we want predictable movement: gentle zoom, light sweep, rain, glow, pulse, or parallax-like movement.

Most controllable Good for final art Needs frames

2. gifski encoder

Cleaner GIF quality

gifski does not invent motion by itself. It makes an existing frame sequence look smoother and cleaner as a GIF.

Best colors Smoother GIF Still needs frames

3. ImageMagick

Simple frame stepping

Good for quick experiments, resizing, looping, and simple frame assembly. Motion can feel more mechanical.

Quick Simple Less polished

4. ComfyUI / AnimateDiff

AI-interpreted motion

Best for expressive animation, but it may change details, faces, wall text, or layout unless constrained carefully.

Most alive Least predictable Heavier setup
